Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start learning English here by practicing daily, learning basic words, reading short sentences, asking questions, correcting mistakes, and speaking aloud for at least fifteen minutes every day.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes, learning and using ChatGPT here is free. You can ask questions, practice English, get explanations, translations, and exercises anytime without payment.
3. Do I need to create an account?
Yes, you need to create an account to use this website. Creating an account allows you to save chats, get better help, and access learning features easily.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
You can build your English vocabulary by learning new words daily, reading simple texts, using words in sentences, practicing regularly, and asking for meanings, examples, and corrections on this website.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
No, I don’t provide official certificates. But I can help you learn, practice, and improve your English step by step.
